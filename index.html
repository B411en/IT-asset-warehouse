    System Operational Guide

    Welcome to the Asia Aluminium IT Control Suite.

    This platform serves two tightly unified structural functions: issuing formal printable corporate asset receipts, and processing logs whenever hardware flows back into storage.

    🔑 Multi-Tier Access Rule By default, the system boots in View-Only Mode. Anyone can inspect logs or draft a form for localized printing. However, committing records permanently to the Firebase Live Archive, wiping indexes, or altering operational structures requires authenticating via the IT Admin Login control at the top menu.
